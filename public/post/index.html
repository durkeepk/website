satRdays: an introduction

I’m planning to make a (semi-)weekly habit of learning a new thing in R (e.g., a function, an analysis, a feature) and writing a breif overview/tutorial of it. This will be the first of the series. The main motivation for starting this series is to contribute to the community that helped me—and continues to help me—learn R. Great resources for learning R are already scattered across the internet, but I figure one more voice couldn’t hurt.
